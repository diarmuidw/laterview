{% trans "What is FilterCam.com?" %}

Network cameras work on your internet and are easy to set up and see the image produced
But then you go to work and you can't see the camera with your computer. ( We like firewalls!!)
However, these cameras all have FTP upload which means they can push images up to the internet.
But most people don't have a place to store these images and if they do, no easy way to view them.
FilterCam.com creates an FTP username and password unique to your camera. You can then set up your camera to use these credentials
Once your camera is uploading, you browse to FilterCam.com from anywhere in the world, login and and see your images.

Your images are secure.
